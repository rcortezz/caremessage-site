About CareMessage

CareMessage uses technology to address challenges for society's most vulnerable populations. Over 86% of low income individuals in the US have access to a basic cell phone with messaging capabilities, making it one of the great social equalizers of our time. CareMessage's technology enables healthcare and social services agencies to better communicate and engage with their beneficiaries using mobile technology, improving overall health outcomes and dramatically reducing cost of care. We're growing at a staggering rate and want to work with the absolute best. Come change the world and make a real impact on healthcare!

In addition, you’ll work with our Scientific Advisory Board, a group of top leaders from the academic and industry side of healthcare. We think this could be an amazing opportunity for an exceptional, motivated candidate.

  • Augment the existing research pipeline

    • Investigate researchers and topics already existing in the literature as options for future independent partners
    • Organize, synthesize and disseminate findings from the literature on SMS and health

  • Ideally 2-3 years of experience working in academics, biotech, pharmaceuticals, or in a health-technology company with a research focus
  • Understanding of research concepts such as study design, independent variables, statistical analyses, sources of bias, etc.
  • You are excited by tough questions that require a lot of digging to find the answer
  • Comfortable reading and disseminating information from peer-reviewed articles

  • Provide recommendations for product enhancements based on configuration/implementation experience.

  • Manage project milestones, progress, and issues.

  • Bachelor's Degree with 3 - 4 years of customer-facing and/or project management experience.

  • Strong technical background with experience in building and using REST APIs.
  • Strong project management skills.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving ability.
  • Creativity and the ability to identify unique solutions for our customers.
